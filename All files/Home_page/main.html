Things to do

Top Destinations

Sigiriya

Rising from the lush jungle cover, Sigiriya Rock is truly a breathtaking sight to behold.Hiking to the top of the Sigiriya Rock Fortress to explore the ancient ruins and enjoy the vistas over the misty forests, lakes and villages.

Galle

Galle is the place to go when you need to return to a simpler pace of life. The best months to travel Galle are between December to April Top tourist tractions are Galle Fort, Beaches and Whale Watching.

Kandy

Kandy is famous for it's spiritual and historical significance. Best time to visit Kandy is December to April. Top tourist trractions are Royal Botanical Garden, Kandy Lake and Temple Of The Tooth.

Ella

Ella is famous for Hikes, mountain views and train rides.The greatest time to visit Ella is from January to March because there are fewer chances of rain and you can go hiking during that time.

Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura is famous for Bodhi tree and beautifully carved stone structures. Beast time to visit is September and April. Top tourist attractions are Singiriya, Ruwanwelisaya and Mihintale.

Colombo

Colombo is famous for Its national museum and temples. The greatest time to visit Colombo is from January to March. Galle Face, Gangaramaya Temple and Independence Memorial Park are the top attractions.
